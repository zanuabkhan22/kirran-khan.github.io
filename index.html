Research Projects

Socio-cultural Causes of Spousal Abuse

An in-depth analysis of the cultural and societal factors contributing to women's vulnerability and spousal abuse in the specific region of Warah Sehran Wala.

Sociological Analysis of Widowhood

Case Study: South Punjab, Pakistan

Research exploring the social, economic, and psychological challenges faced by widows in South Punjab, highlighting their resilience and societal role.

An ethnographic study commissioned by the German Embassy, examining the dynamics of interfaith harmony and the promotion of peace across different communities in Sindh and Punjab.
